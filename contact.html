Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a waste pickup?

Register as a seller, complete your profile, and use our waste collection page to schedule pickups at your convenience.

What types of waste do you accept?

We accept plastic, paper, metal, glass, electronics, and organic waste. Check our recycling guide for specific details.

How are prices determined for recyclables?

Prices are based on current market rates, material quality, and quantity. You'll see real-time pricing in your seller dashboard.

Is there a minimum quantity for pickup?

Minimum quantities vary by material type. Generally, we require at least 10kg for most materials to make pickup economical.
